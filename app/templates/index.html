CryptoIOC

CryptoIOC has the goal of sharing IP/port pairs used to connect to cryptomining pools.

Why? because more and more malware install a stealthy cryptominer. Infected systems are then (ab)used and have their resources sucked to mine cryptocurrencies for miscreants.
